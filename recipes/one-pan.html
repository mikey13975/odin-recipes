Easy Skillet Chicken Primavera

Easiest Chicken Primavera You Evermade

Quick, easy, stick to your ribs goodness will have the whole family asking for seconds. And with only one pan to clean, you can spend more time relaxing after a long day at work.

Ingredients

  • 2 tblsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups asperagus, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomato, halved
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cups penne pasta
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over high heat.
  2. Cook garlic and chicken until no pink is showing
  3. Add asparagus, tomatoes, carrots, salt, and pepper, cooking for about two minutes.
  4. Add pasta and parmesan, stirring until cheese is melted and evenly distributed
  5. Serve
  6. Enjoy!!!
